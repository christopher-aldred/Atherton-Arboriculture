What We Do

Dedicated to every aspect of tree maintenance

Once we have established your tree related problem, we strive to offer you the most suitable solution using a range of arboricultural practices. During any of our arboricultural operations we always look to minimise disruption and damage to your tree's surroundings using a variety of methods such as rigging and lowering of branches or placing protection in place where required.

About the owner

Atherton Arboricultural Services is owned and operated by William Atherton who has over 5 years experience within the industry and also has a FdSc in Arboriculture and Urban Forestry through Myerscough and is in the process of completing his full Bsc in Arboriculture and Urban Forestry. He has worked all over the UK, but mostly operates in the North West of England and is based out of Preston. He also sub contracts out as a climber to multiple firms across the North West
